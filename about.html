✦ Our Story ✦

Founded in 2018

Bella Monroe Bridal was born from a love of timeless elegance and the belief that every bride deserves a dress that tells her story. Our Brisbane boutique offers a curated selection of gowns and a personalised fitting experience unlike any other.

We believe that finding your wedding dress should be one of the most joyful experiences of your life. That's why we limit appointments, train our stylists to truly listen, and carefully curate our collections to represent a range of silhouettes, fabrics, and price points.

From your first appointment to the final fitting, we are by your side every step of the way.
